Social Responsibility
Renew with Jambu™

Inspired by the beauty and mystique of the rainforest, Jambu is an outdoor inspired footwear brand that uses recycled, recyclable, and re-usable packaging. Jambu™ outsoles are made with partially recycled and re-usable compressed rubber.

To reinforce our mutual love and respect for nature, Jambu™ has partnered with American Forests to aid in the preservation and restoration of forests devastated by wildfires as a result of natural and manmade disasters.

American Forests is the oldest natual conservation non-profit in the United States. Jambu has committed to plant 50,000 trees across five U.S. forests targeted for restoration. In this small way we hope to preserve our grat outdoors for future generations.

"Jambu is a tree that grows in the rainforest and we wanted to do our part in giving back to the place that defines us. Like the forest, Jambu prides itself on being ecofriendly, made for the outdoors and great for the soul/sole." - Yetzalee Cubero, Director of Marketing, Jambu
